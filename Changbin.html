MEMBERS DISCOGROPHY STORYLINE

CHANGBIN

Birthday: August 11th 1999

Position / sub unit: 3racha

Personality Type: ENFP-T

Representative Animal: ⅔ Pig, ⅓ Rabbit

Birth name: 서창빈 (Seo Chang Bin)

Changbin was born in Yongin, South Korea. In school, he studied really hard, and wanted to become really successful, however at one point he stopped studying for a while, and started listening to rap music, after he had performed in front of his school, and realized that he had a huge talent for rapping he decided to make a few auditions for entertainment companies, and got accepted into a lot of them, but ultimately ended up choosing JYP entertainment. He worked really hard in order to be able to make his debut, eventually meeting BangChan and was added to 3racha. After competing in the Stray Kids reality competition show, he ended up debuting in Stray Kids, together with the rest of the members.
