NOEASY is their most recent full length album, with the name of the album being a play on the word ‘Noisy’, as that word usually is used when describing Stray Kids music, and not always in a positive way, so with this album they decided to turn those negative comments into something positive, and basically say “yeah… we are noisy and we are proud of that”. The title-track ‘Thunderous’ is especially noisy, with many background noises that makes the song special.
