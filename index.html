ABOUT STRAY KIDS

Stray kids is a South Korean boygroup under JYP entertainment. The group consists of 8 members, BangChan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. They made their debut back in 2018 with the mini album titled “I am NOT”, but it wasn’t until two years later with their first full album “GO LIVE” they finally reached huge success. With their producing sub unit ‘3racha’, they make almost 100% of their music, with only a few songs being touched by anybody outside of Stray Kids.
