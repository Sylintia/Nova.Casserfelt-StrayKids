MEMBERS DISCOGROPHY STORYLINE

BANGCHAN

Birthday: October 3rd 1997

Position / sub unit: Leader, 3racha

Personality Type: ENFJ-T

Representative Animal: Wolf

Birth name: Christopher Bahng

Korean name: 방찬 (Bang Chan)

BangChan was born in Seoul, South Korea, but moved to Sydney, Australia when he was very young. One day when his mother picked him up from school, she asked him if he would like to do an audition for JYP entertainment, since he was really interested in music from a very young age. He passed the audition and moved back to South Korea in 2010. In 2016, he and two other members, Changbin and Han, created a rap group they called 3racha, which later on became the producing sub unit of Stray kids. He was asked by the company to pick out a few trainees from the company he would like to debut with, and ended up picking the other stray kids members, and naturally became the leader of the group. In 2017, Stray kids was presented to the world through their reality competition show “Stray Kids”, and made their official debut on the 25th of March 2018, and BangChan finally made his debut after 7 years of being a trainee.

LEE KNOW

Birthday: October 25th 1998

Position / sub unit: Dance Racha

Personality Type: ESFJ-T

Representative Animal: Rabbit

Birth name: 이민호 (Lee Min Ho)

Lee Know was born in Gimpo, South Korea. Growing up, he was very interested in dancing, and very talented at that. He worked as a professional dancer before becoming a kpop idol, and was a backup dancer for BTS on their Japanese tour. He was called to a JYP entertainment audition, but didn’t hear anything from them after, so naturally he thought he had failed. However, the company called back after a few years and asked him to become a trainee, which he did. After a year in the company, the Stray Kids survival show began, which he was sadly eliminated from in the third episode, but was added back into the group in the last episode.

CHANGBIN

Birthday: August 11th 1999

Position / sub unit: 3racha

Personality Type: ENFP-T

Representative Animal: ⅔ Pig, ⅓ Rabbit

Birth name: 서창빈 (Seo Chang Bin)

Changbin was born in Yongin, South Korea. In school, he studied really hard, and wanted to become really successful, however at one point he stopped studying for a while, and started listening to rap music, after he had performed in front of his school, and realized that he had a huge talent for rapping he decided to make a few auditions for entertainment companies, and got accepted into a lot of them, but ultimately ended up choosing JYP entertainment. He worked really hard in order to be able to make his debut, eventually meeting BangChan and was added to 3racha. After competing in the Stray Kids reality competition show, he ended up debuting in Stray Kids, together with the rest of the members.

HYUNJIN

Birthday: March 20th 2000

Position / sub unit: Dance Racha

Personality Type: INFP-T

Representative Animal: Ferret

Birth name: 황현진 (Hwang Hyun Jin)

Hyunjin was born in Seoul, South Korea, but lived in Las Vegas for a few months when he was 6 years old. In 2015, he got scouted by a JYP employee when out shopping with his mom. After looking into more information about the company, and made sure it wasn’t a scam, he ended up joining the company. At first, he didn’t really have a lot of talent in dancing, rapping or singing, and even started hating dancing, he got told that he didn’t need talents to debut because he is good looking, which only pushed him to training harder, and eventually got really good at all the qualities he previously felt insecure about, especially dancing. He was added into the Stray kids lineup after making it through the competition show, and made his debut in 2018 with the other members.

HAN

Birthday: September 14th 2000

Position / sub unit: 3racha

Personality Type: ISTP-T

Representative Animal: Quokka

Birth name: 한지성 (Han Ji Sung)

Han was born in Incheon, South Korea, but ended up moving to Malaysia after a while. He was always interested in singing, and felt as though he wouldn’t be able to pursue his dream in Malaysia, and made a deal with his parents that if he didn’t get accepted to an entertainment company within a year in Korea, he would come back and continue his studies. He ended up going to a JYP audition with his friend, just in case they would see a celebrity, even though he didn’t think he would get accepted. However he got accepted, and was the first person to meet BangChan and is the youngest member of 3racha, together with Bangchan and Changbin. After making it through the Stray Kids competition, he made his official debut.

FELIX

Birthday: September 15th 2000

Position / sub unit: Dance Racha

Personality Type: ENFP-T

Representative Animal: Chick

Birth name: Felix Lee

Korean name: 이용복 (Lee Yong Bok)

Felix was born in Sydney, Australia. He randomly decided one day that he wanted to become a dancer, although his parents didn’t approve at first, he ended up making an audition for JYP entertainment, and got accepted. He moved to South Korea when he was 16 years old, without knowing much Korean at all. After BangChan found out that there was another Australian trainee, he immediately added Felix to the potential Stray Kids group. As the reality show premiered, Felix sadly got eliminated on the sixth episode due to his lack of knowing the Korean language, and barely being able to communicate, but just like Lee Know, was added back into the group in the last episode, as he had worked really hard on improving his Korean.

SEUNGMIN

Birthday: September 22nd 2000

Position / sub unit: Vocal Racha

Personality Type: ESFJ-A

Representative Animal: Dog

Birth name: 김승민 (Kim Seung Min)/p>

Seungmin was born in Seoul, South Korea. When he was in fourth grade, he lived in Los Angeles for 3 months, but moved back to South Korea. Growing up, he really enjoyed playing baseball, but was forced to quit. He had always liked singing, and had always dreamt of joining JYP entertainment, so after placing 2nd place in a JYP open audition, he got accepted into the company. He worked really hard trying to make it to debut, and ended up making his dream come true after making it through the Stray Kids show, and becoming a vocalist in the group.

I.N

Birthday: February 8th 2001

Position / sub unit: Maknae, Vocal Racha

Personality Type: ISFJ

Representative Animal: Fennec Fox

Birth name: 양정인 (Yang Jeong In)

I.N was born in Busan, South Korea. His passion for music was clear from a very young age, and he learned the Korean traditional way of singing called Trot, which he used to perform to the elders in his town. After deciding to become a singer, he made a deal with his parents that he had to get accepted into a company before he graduated middle school, otherwise he would give up on that dream and go back to studying full time. After making an audition to JYP entertainment and failing, they called back at a later date and asked him to come do another audition, which he passed this time. He debuted as the youngest member (the maknae) of Stray Kids after 2 years of training, and after making it through the competition show.
